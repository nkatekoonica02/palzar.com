The Palzar clothing history


Palzar clothing was established in 2010, 2 months before world cup. In 2010 Palzar clothing started by only personalizing towels, pyjamas, bath ropes and face cloths.


This business was located in Mabopane block C from 2010 to 2016 ,then relocated to Soshanguve block XX from 2016-2019.

Palzar, then relocated to Pretoria West since 2019.Palzar has been situated in Madeira Isles till current date.
Palzar is currently for black-owned 100%.

It is a female company and we have ventured into skills development for the past 4 years.
Which is a training centre for sewing and alteration, basically everything you need to know about designing clothes.

Designing has always been a primary goal from bath towels to school uniform to designing clothing. Our main service is designing uniform and training.
